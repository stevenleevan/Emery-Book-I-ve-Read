On the Shelf

  • Green Ember Series

    S.D. Smith

    Rabbit warriors fighting against a hawk army lead by a hawk named Morbin Blackhawk.

  • Wing Feather Saga Series

    Andrew Peterson

    Funny, deep, and exciting. The world is so amazing and there's a lot of mystery and a little bit of magic.

  • Dragon Masters Series

    Tracey West

    Great quick reads with dragons, each book feels like a fun adventure in a magical world.

  • The Secret Series

    Psevdonymovs Bosch

    It was absolutely amazing. I liked the secrets and the narrator talking to me.

  • Mysterious Benedict Society Series

    Trenton Lee Stewart

    Kids solving problems. I liked how each one had such different character traits.

  • Harry Potter Series

    J. K. Rowling

    Magic, school, and danger. It was the perfect series for me. I couldnt stop reading once I started, I would just lay in my bed reading and reading and reading.
